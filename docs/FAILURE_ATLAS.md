# Failure Atlas: Rocq LLM-Tooling Experiment
*Synthesized from 17 finding groups covering 43 attempts across 10 experimental conditions (winner/haiku incremental, sonnet incremental, sonnet naive, rocqmcp, context ablations, team-of-agents).*

---

## 1. Executive Summary: The Deepest Mechanisms

**1. The gap between "proof found" and "proof scored" is mostly plumbing, not mathematics.**
The single largest quantified loss in the entire dataset is a finalization handshake: `auto_close` closes the last goal but never says Qed is required, so agents declare victory and exit with no candidate file. This hit 13 attempts across 7 problems in the sonnet-incremental run ÔÇö arithmetically the *entire* ~10pp gap versus the naive interface, where a whole-file candidate with `Qed` exists by construction.

> Tool: `` `lra.` closes the current goal ÔÇö COMMITTED. goals: 0. No more goals.`` Ôćĺ Agent: `"PROOF COMPLETE.\n\nDONE"` ÔÇö server.jsonl: `"closed":true, "complete":false`, work/ empty. In 12 of 13 cases the agent *fabricated* the words "PROOF COMPLETE." that the tool never emitted.

**2. The Lean dialect tax is the dominant turn sink in every configuration.**
Haiku-4-5 (and to a lesser degree Sonnet) natively writes Lean 4/Mathlib ÔÇö `nlinarith`, `rw [h]`, `have ... := by`, `obtain ÔčĘÔčę`, `sorry` ÔÇö plausibly primed by the `lean_workbook_*` problem names. Each Lean-ism costs a full turn against the same opaque parser error, reactive hints correct one alias per turn, and the model relapses the next turn. Measured cost: 10ÔÇô20 of ~30 calls per attempt across at least 12 of 17 groups; ~24/45 calls in one team attempt died on the identical error.

> One agent, after proving to itself that bare tactics fail: *"Let me try without the period"* ÔÇö reasoning exactly backwards. Another re-emitted `nlinarith` **five times** after being explicitly hinted it is Lean.

**3. The interface actively recommends tools that deterministically cannot run.**
`psatz` ÔÇö the designed tactic for these Positivstellensatz-shaped inequalities, listed in both the error hints and the `auto_close` portfolio ÔÇö *always* fails because the csdp binary is not installed. `field_simp` is advertised twice in the portfolio message yet is not importable under the loaded prefix. Agents in at least 6 groups burned turns rediscovering this.

> `psatz R 4.` ÔÇö *"Tactic failure: ... requires ... CSDP. However, the "csdp" binary is not present in the search path"* ÔÇö while the server's own hint says *"use `nra` or `psatz R 2`"*.

**4. False and hard are indistinguishable, and agents inherit that blindness.**
`lra`/`nra` say "No applicable tactic" and `ring` says "not a valid ring equation" identically for unprovable goals and merely-hard ones. Consequence: a 15-turn spiral proving the false subgoal `1 - a > 0`, a sign-flipped identity pursued to death, one agent proving the contradiction `a+b+c+1=0` as a lemma instead of exploiting it under `exfalso` ÔÇö while a *true* key assert was abandoned after a single nra failure.

> `assert (H1 : (a-1)*(b-1)*(c-1) > -1). nra.` ÔÇö the correct fact, abandoned; final action: *"rolled back 15 sentence(s). 0 remain committed."*

**5. "no_candidate" is a censoring artifact: most failures died mid-viable-proof at a hard cap.**
Nearly every incremental failure is `error_max_turns` at exactly 30/31 turns with a correct plan in flight. Readers verified by replay: 25432 was **3 tactics from Qed** (`rewrite <- Zmult_mod. rewrite Z.mul_1_l. reflexivity.` compiles); R_rm was **one `f_equal`** from Qed; 69551 was 2ÔÇô3 turns out; two hard problems have compiled 5ÔÇô25 sentence proofs using only the tools present. Both winner-medium agents hit the cap *one turn after* finally acquiring the missing lemma.

**6. Full-file context wins through proof *idioms* from adjacent bodies ÔÇö Search already finds the statements.**
In the context ablation, lean-context agents successfully discovered every lemma the reference proofs use (CRltLinear, plus_IZR family, Qred_correct). What statement-only context withheld was *how proofs in this file go*: the midpoint-instantiation trick, `constructor; try easy`, the `Qabs_Qlt_condition ... lra` closing recipe, even correct parenthesization ÔÇö each present verbatim in the immediately preceding, unseen proof bodies. Full-context agents harvested external lemma names *from inside neighboring proof bodies*. The context-dependent delta lives in ~100ÔÇô300 lines, not 47K tokens. (And one of three full-context "wins" used no context at all ÔÇö `auto_close` closed it in 2.6 ms.)

**7. Two configurations never enter the verify loop at all.**
rocqmcp agents submitted blind Lean-dialect proofs within 4ÔÇô22 seconds because a startup race left only `mcp__final__submit` visible (rocqmcp "pending") and submit accepted anything with *"Submitted... Reply DONE."* ÔÇö `recompile_failed` really means *never compiled*. Sonnet-naive burned entire 300s budgets in single unbroken extended-thinking streams: 9 of 13 timeouts had **zero tool calls** (transcripts are literally init + 150ÔÇô220 thinking events reaching 20ÔÇô30k tokens), while the identical problem solved on the sibling rep in 113s with 3 sub-500ms tool calls.

## 2. Findings by Question

### winner_hard_fail ÔÇö Why does the winning config fail hard problems?
**Not because they are out of reach.** Of 6 hard failures, readers verified 4ÔÇô5 were provable in 5ÔÇô25 sentences with exactly the tools present (two compiled witnesses: `/tmp/wtest/t46206.v`, `/tmp/wtest/t12243.v`); only 66169 required mathematical insight (mod-17 case analysis) the model never produced. The failure recipe is: one early strategic commitment (a witness schema, an SOS decomposition, a factorization) that must be *found and trusted*, sabotaged by (a) false-vs-hard indistinguishable errors letting sign errors survive and false branches consume half the budget, (b) a broken nonlinear backend (csdp dead, nra's real envelope ÔÇö pairwise hypothesis products only ÔÇö unknown to the agent), and (c) a 30ÔÇô50% turn tax from Lean churn and non-atomic multi-sentence steps that leave unmatched `{` focus blocks (~10 turns + 2 rollbacks in one attempt). All six died at the turn cap mid-proof. Notably, 52181 was mathematically solved on **turn 1** and 81971's contradiction was literally on screen at death.

### winner_med_fail ÔÇö Why does it fail medium problems?
**Turn-budget death from friction, not missing math.** All five attempts ended at exactly `error_max_turns` with monotone progress and verified-short proofs available (25432: 3 tactics out; both agents acquired their missing key lemma ÔÇö `pow2_ge_0`, `Z.mul_mod` ÔÇö on their final turns). Budget breakdown, in order: Lean syntax churn (10ÔÇô17 failed calls per attempt), dead advertised tooling (psatz/field_simp), and lemma-surface friction ÔÇö `Search (_ ^ 2 >= 0)` returns *nothing* though `pow2_ge_0` exists as `0 <= x ^ 2` (verified), Rsqr `┬▓` vs `^ 2` unification failures, Mathlib name guessing. Fixing deterministic friction alone converts 2ÔÇô3 of these 5.

### winner_hard_solve ÔÇö What actually carries the wins?
**The server, overwhelmingly.** In 2 of 3 hard solves, `auto_close` alone closed the goal on the first call (plain `lra`; a 20-line server-*generated* square-hint + `nra` script) ÔÇö the agent contributed only `Qed`, and the "hard" label collapsed under the finisher portfolio. In the one agent-driven solve, the win came from the interaction loop: auto_close's failure coaching ("Do structural work... and try again") steered the decomposition, try's prefix-pass feedback separated a correct skeleton from a wrong finisher, and Search + error hints supplied every lemma name after repeated Lean relapses. Load-bearing ranking: **auto_close portfolio + hint synthesis Ôëź try prefix-feedback + auto-commit Ôëź error hints Ôëź Search.** That solve was one hint-vocabulary gap (goal-subterm square harvesting) from being another 1-call auto_close.

### sonnet_incremental_fail ÔÇö Does the step-wise flow derail Sonnet's global plans?
**No ÔÇö decisively.** In every long attempt Sonnet stated a correct global strategy (SOS/Schur certificate; cross-multiply-then-conclude) and drove it linearly. The ~10pp loss versus naive decomposes into: (1) the finalize handshake trap (13/20 failed attempts; 6 problems lost on both reps = the whole gap); (2) wall-clock kills of coherent in-flight proofs that had used **0.7ÔÇô3.1s of prover time in 300s** ÔÇö single model stalls of 67ÔÇô225s consumed the budget; (3) an `auto_close` bug committing always-succeeding no-ops (*"`auto with real arith.` closes the current goal ÔÇö COMMITTED. goals: 1"*, goal unchanged) costing diagnose-and-rollback turns; (4) a fixed prefix that pins imports, making a correct cast-to-Z plan unimplementable, followed by a ~15k-token no-tool thinking spiral until watchdog kill.

### sonnet_naive_timeout ÔÇö What do the naive timeouts look like?
**Think-to-death before the first action.** 9 of 13 timeouts have `tool_calls=0`: one unbroken extended-thinking generation (20ÔÇô30k tokens at normal streaming speed ÔÇö no API stall) deriving closed forms and SOS certificates in-head instead of exploiting a 0.3ÔÇô0.5s check loop. Rep-level contrast proves stochastic budget allocation, not difficulty: 23587 rep0 timed out at 30.4k thinking tokens and 0 calls; rep1 solved the same statement in 113.7s. The only compile-wait pathology is one problem (66199, both reps): `vm_compute` on unary-nat `99^49`-scale literals, which categorically cannot terminate and deterministically eats the 60s compile timeout.

### rocqmcp_badsubmit ÔÇö What broke between session and artifact?
**Nothing broke; the file was never compiled.** In all five attempts, `work/candidate.v` is byte-identical to the (first and only) submit payload; the gate's fresh recompile was the first compile the file ever received, dying at the first Lean-ism (`ÔčĘ` lexer error, `have ... := by` parse error). Three attempts: startup race ÔÇö rocqmcp "pending", tool list contained only `mcp__final__submit`, agent submitted blind in 2 turns. Two attempts: submit-as-*first*-action, then verify after the fact ÔÇö in 27783 the agent's own `rocq_compile` reproduced the gate error **byte-for-byte one call after submitting**, then made real interactive progress (found the correct `sin_eq_0_0/1` lemmas) that was never re-serialized into a new submission before the 31-turn cap. The unconditional success string *"Submitted... Reply DONE."* terminates weak agents instantly.

### rocqmcp_nosubmit ÔÇö Was progress made but left unsubmitted?
**Mostly no ÔÇö the failure is upstream of submission.** No attempt ever produced a compiling proof, so there was never anything to submit; all four were terminated externally (turn cap at ~85s, or watchdog after three diverging computation calls ate ~236/301s and a pet restart expired the session state). Turn sinks: the Lean loop (identical mistake recommitted up to 7 times); a fixed 3-turn opening tax (`rocq_start` naming/validation failures, then compile-with-sorry as a state-harvesting workaround); a silent no-op trap (`rocq_check` returning `success:true, commands_run:0` on unparseable bodies, manufacturing the false belief *"decide seems to work"*); and `rocq_query` dropping `Open Scope Z_scope`, blocking the exact witness computation that would have finished 57775. The one exception: 69551 had genuine verified progress (correct lemma in context, ~2ÔÇô3 turns from Qed) stranded by the cap.

### ctx_full_med_solve ÔÇö What mechanism does full context actually provide?
**Two separable mechanisms, only one of which needs the context.** (A) Lemma-name recall by statement match: firing `exact plus_IZR` etc. on subgoals that verbatim-match earlier in-file lemma statements. (B) Proof-idiom transfer from adjacent proof *bodies*: the local skeleton (`apply Rquot1` + `Rrepr_*` rewriting), scope discipline (`Open Scope CReal_scope`), and external lemma names harvested from inside a neighbor's proof (`CReal_inv_l` from Rrepr_inv's body). One of three solves (Rinv_l rep0) used **no context at all** ÔÇö auto_close won in 2.6 ms. The context-dependent delta is concentrated in the few hundred lines immediately preceding the target, and mechanism (A) is mechanizable server-side.

### ctx_lean_med_fail ÔÇö Did lean-context agents fail to discover?
**No ÔÇö discovery worked; dialect and idiom were withheld.** In all four attempts, Search/Check/Print assembled *every* ingredient of the reference proof (CRltLinear + its linearity witness; all four IZR lemmas; cauchy/Qred_correct). Deaths came from missing proof idiom ÔÇö the midpoint instantiation `destruct (s r2 r1 r3 Hlt)`, `constructor; try easy` + `f_equal; apply Z.eqb_eq`, the `Qabs_Qlt_condition ... lra` closer ÔÇö each present verbatim in the unseen adjacent proofs ÔÇö compounded by the Lean tax and pattern-Search blindness to facts inside product-typed structure fields. R_rm died one `f_equal` from Qed; CRle_lt_trans died on unparenthesized application (`exact Hr12 c.` vs `exact (Hr12 c).`) with the proof semantically complete.

### team_fail ÔÇö Where does team wall-clock go?
**Syntax churn, duplication, and degenerate topology ÔÇö almost none to proof search.** ~36/46 and ~24/45 step/try calls in live attempts died at parse on Lean-isms. Phases duplicate rather than compound: every phase opens with state + auto_close on the identical goal, and finishers replay workers' failed try-portfolios verbatim because no attempted-tactics ledger crosses phase boundaries. Topology was degenerate both ways: one coordinator produced a genuine 3-way split but only 1 worker spawned and a goal stayed permanently *"owned by coord"*; elsewhere 3 phases processed 1 sequential goal three times. A coordinator's `cut G` manufactured the tautology `G Ôćĺ G`, so an entire worker's contribution was closing an artifact of the coordinator's mistake. The auto_close false positive misled all three phases per attempt. One attempt was a pure API-outage washout: 299s of serial retry ladders, zero tool calls ÔÇö phases spawned even after the prior phase did nothing. The best vein (7839's finisher, 3 committed sentences, ~3 from a candidate) was cut off by a fixed turn budget and discarded as `no_candidate`.

## 3. Consolidated Tool Recommendations (priority-ordered, deduplicated)

1. **Auto-Qed + eager candidate checkpointing.** [deterministic/server-side/zero-turn] When any commit reaches 0 goals, run `Qed` in the same call, write `work/candidate.v`, and return "PROOF COMPLETE ÔÇö file saved. Reply DONE." Also checkpoint the committed prefix (+`Admitted` if incomplete) after every commit so watchdog/max-turns kills leave a gradeable artifact. *Evidence: 13 attempts / ~10pp in sonnet-incremental; every no_candidate max-turns death (team_fail 7839, ctx_lean R_rm, rocqmcp 69551).*

2. **Server-side LeanÔćĺRocq auto-repair before parsing, not reactive hints after.** [deterministic/server-side/zero-turn] Append missing terminal `.`; transpile the fixed table (`rw [h]`Ôćĺ`rewrite h`; `have h : T := by tac`Ôćĺ`assert (h:T). tac.`; `nlinarith [e..]`Ôćĺassert hints + `nra`; `omega`Ôćĺ`lia`; `sorry`Ôćĺ`admit`; `simp at h`Ôćĺ`simpl in h`; `sq_nonneg`Ôćĺ`pow2_ge_0`; unicode `ÔčĘÔčę`Ôćĺdestruct patterns); auto-parenthesize `exact f x.`Ôćĺ`exact (f x).` on the ltac_use_default error; report "auto-repaired: ÔÇŽ". *Evidence: 10ÔÇô20 dead calls per attempt in Ôëą12 groups; two attempts died solely on parenthesization; the current hint pipeline demonstrably does not prevent relapse.*

3. **Make the arithmetic backend honest: install csdp, or strip psatz/field_simp everywhere they're advertised.** [deterministic/server-side/zero-turn] psatz currently *always* fails; field_simp is listed twice in the portfolio yet unimportable. Either install csdp (+ `Require Import Field` in R prefixes) or purge them from hints and the auto_close roster. *Evidence: dead psatz calls in 6 groups; 42964 plausibly closes inside the existing auto_close sweep with csdp present.*

4. **Compile-gate `submit`; fix the MCP startup race.** [deterministic/server-side/zero-turn] Run the gate's fresh recompile inside submit and return the compiler error on failure; lexically reject `sorry/admit`; optionally require a hash matching the last successful `rocq_compile`; never emit an unconditional "Reply DONE"; block the agent's first turn until rocqmcp reports connected; retain the last fully-compiling source as fallback artifact. *Evidence: all 5 rocqmcp_badsubmit attempts; 29666 and 27783 were directly convertible.*

5. **Fix auto_close winner detection.** [deterministic/server-side/zero-turn] A candidate "closes" only if goal count strictly decreases (closers must reach 0); never commit no-ops; exclude `auto with ...`; report which finisher got furthest. *Evidence: false "`auto with real arith.` closes it ÔÇö COMMITTED, goals: 1" misled agents in 5+ attempts across sonnet_incremental and team_fail, each costing multi-turn reconciliation.*

6. **Falsifiability feedback on failure: counterexample sampler + ring-diff.** [deterministic/server-side/zero-turn] On failed lra/nra/assert goals, numerically sample the hypothesis box and report "goal FALSIFIED at a=1.5, b=0.5, c=0.5" or "no counterexample in N samples"; on `ring` failure over a well-formed equality, print `ring_simplify(LHSÔłĺRHS)`'s nonzero normal form instead of "try field/lra/nra". *Evidence: 46206 rep1's 15-turn false branch and abandoned true assert; rep0's sign error; 81971's vacuity (add a hypothesis-satisfiability probe: "hypotheses may be inconsistent ÔÇö consider exfalso").*

7. **Extend the finisher portfolio and re-run it after every commit.** [deterministic/server-side/zero-turn] Add: a positivity chain (`repeat (apply Rplus_le_le_0_compat || Rmult_le_pos || pow2_ge_0 || lra)`); goal-subterm square harvesting (assert `0 <= t^2` for every `t^2` in the goal, not just variables); sum-splitting for `╬úXi >= 0`; a Z-divisibility residue enumerator for `(p | e)` with small prime p; Rge/Rle normalization (`apply Rle_ge` pre-pass); and re-fire the portfolio automatically when the goal shape changes (e.g., becomes division-free). Also run it once at proof start and at budget exhaustion. *Evidence: would one-call 52181; closes 66169-class mechanically; 26869 built the exact psatz-shaped subgoal at turn 26 with auto_close last run at turn 2; R_rm and CRle_lt_trans were closable by `assumption/contradiction` at death.*

8. **Goal-aware lemma injection + Search hardening.** [deterministic/server-side/zero-turn] On each new subgoal, scan the compiled prefix for statement-matching lemmas and append "exact-match in-file lemmas: ÔÇŽ" (mechanizes the full-context recall mechanism); normalize Search orientation (`>=` Ôćö `<=`, unfold Rsqr/pow aliases ÔÇö verified: `Search (_ ^ 2 >= 0)` returns nothing though `pow2_ge_0` exists); search inside structure-field types on 0 hits (transitivity hidden in `isLinearOrder`); auto-correct malformed patterns; cap output at top-K ranked by goal-symbol overlap (a 112KB dump hit an unreadable overflow file); print an explicit "(no results)"; map Mathlib names in near-miss lists. *Evidence: winner_med (both), ctx_lean (all), winner_hard_solve.*

9. **Atomic multi-sentence step + prefix auto-commit in try.** [deterministic/server-side/zero-turn] On mid-block error, auto-rollback the partial commit (never leave an unmatched `{`); auto-insert `}` when a focused subgoal closes; offer one-token commit of try's "(N sentence(s) would pass)" prefix. *Evidence: ~10 turns + 2 rollbacks in 12243; the nested 16-sentence assert accident in 52181; 4 brace-confusion turns in 26869; every attempt re-sent passing prefixes manually.*

10. **Budget telemetry + forced finalization + anti-spiral guards.** [deterministic/server-side/zero-turn] Append `turns_left`/`seconds_left` to every tool result; at ÔëĄ3 turns inject "commit your best candidate / call auto_close now"; idle-nudge after ~60s without a tool call; per-turn streaming deadline (~90s, abort-and-resume with "submit your current best now"); cap extended thinking (~4ÔÇô8k tokens) for the naive config. *Evidence: 9/13 sonnet-naive zero-call timeouts; the 15k-token spiral in 66199; every max-turns death with no submission.*

11. **Divergence guard for computation tactics.** [deterministic/server-side/zero-turn] Instantly refuse `vm_compute/compute/native_compute/auto` on goals with large unary-nat power literals, with the canned fix ("transfer to Z: `Nat2Z.inj_lt` + `Nat2Z.inj_pow`, then vm_compute"). *Evidence: 66199 in three separate configs; ~236/301s in one rocqmcp attempt plus a pet restart that expired all saved states.*

12. **Team orchestration: cross-phase ledger, outage breaker, ownership GC, right-sized fan-out.** [deterministic/server-side/zero-turn] Prepend a "previously failed on this goal: ÔÇŽ" digest to each new phase's first result; skip remaining phases when one exits with zero tool calls (would have saved 209s of the outage washout); release goal ownership on agent exit; spawn min(k, open_subgoals) workers; reject decompositions whose subgoals contain the original goal verbatim (`cut G`); warn on strengthening commits (`constructor.` turning `>=` into `>`); grant a small turn extension (+3) to a finisher whose last step committed sentences.

13. **rocqmcp API ergonomics.** [deterministic/server-side/zero-turn] `rocq_check` must fail loudly (`no_commands_parsed`) when zero sentences parse, never propagating stale `proof_finished`; `rocq_query` inherits the full task prefix (Open Scope included); `rocq_start` validation errors include a concrete valid-call example; alias unprefixed tool names; `Check <tactic>` answers "X is a tactic" instead of crashing.

14. **Environment/prompt construction.** [deterministic/server-side/zero-turn] Preload a standard batteries prefix (ZArith, Lia, Psatz, Field) or accept `Require Import` via transparent prefix rebuild; include the ~100-line prefix tail (preceding proof *bodies*, not just statements) in lean-context prompts and first state results; ship a one-time capability manifest (available decision procedures; "omega/nlinarith/field_simp/norm_num do not exist"); de-prime the Lean prior ("Rocq/Ltac only ÔÇö Lean tactics will not parse"; consider masking `lean_workbook_*` ids).

15. **Budget policy: raise or restructure caps.** [needs-policy] All 14 incremental max-turns deaths were mid-viable-proof; several were 1ÔÇô4 tactics from Qed. Options: higher caps for medium/hard tiers, exempting observation calls (state/Search/Check) from the count, and budgeting by prover time or turns instead of wall clock (killed attempts used <1% of wall clock as prover time).

16. **Policy-side meta-moves.** [needs-policy] Hammer-first discipline (fire lia/nra/psatz via one cheap call before any in-head derivation ÔÇö the think-to-death cure the server cannot fully impose); abandon-the-schema-after-N-failures (66169's linear-witness lock-in survived even self-computed disconfirmation); numeric sanity-checking of conjectured equality points (81971's a=b=c=1 fails its own constraint). Items 6 and 10 provide deterministic scaffolding, but the behavioral change is the model's.

## 4. Limitations of This Sample

- **Small and censored.** 43 attempts total, unevenly split (three tags rest on a single attempt; most groups have 2ÔÇô3). Sampling was failure-focused, so success mechanisms are represented by only 4 solve attempts; base rates and pp-recovery estimates (e.g., "~10pp from auto-Qed") come from one run of one manifest and should not be extrapolated across manifests or models.
- **Single reader per group, no inter-rater check.** Every diagnosis, quote transcription, and turn-accounting figure comes from one analyst reading one group; misattribution (e.g., "would have converted") is unaudited. Where readers *compiled* counterfactual proofs (t12243.v, t46206.v, the 25432 replay, the 42964 two-liner) the claims are strong; the remaining "would_have_helped" claims are plausible but unverified counterfactuals.
- **Correlated evidence.** The same problems recur across groups (66199 in three configs; 12243, 5638, 38038, 42596, 23587 in two each), and most groups share the haiku-4-5 policy, so the 17 groups are far from 17 independent observations. The Lean-contamination finding in particular may be substantially a small-model (and `lean_workbook_*` naming) artifact; Sonnet shows it more weakly and differently.
- **Environment confounds.** One team attempt was a pure API outage; wall-clock budgets confound model latency with proof difficulty; the missing csdp binary and the MCP startup race mean several conditions measured a broken environment rather than the intended interface. Difficulty labels ("hard") are stale relative to the finisher portfolio, as the 1-call auto_close solves demonstrate.
- **Recommendation interactions untested.** The priority ordering assumes fixes are additive; several overlap (auto-repair reduces the value of budget raises; compile-gated submit subsumes the dialect lint), and none has been ablated. Treat the list as a ranked hypothesis set for the next experiment, not measured deltas.
